# 2-Minute Walkthrough Script (Residents / First-Time Users)

Use this as a read-aloud guide while recording your screen.

## Time-Coded Narration Script (120s)

### 0:00-0:12 Intro
**Narration:**  
"This app helps you get clear disaster guidance for your barangay, find evacuation centers, and see emergency hotlines in one place."

### 0:12-0:30 Choose language and barangay
**Narration:**  
"First, choose your preferred language: Tagalog, Bisaya, or English. Then type your barangay and select the correct location."

### 0:30-0:55 Guidance tab
**Narration:**  
"In Guidance, you’ll see plain-language steps for what to do now, in the next 24 hours, during impact, and after. If no active advisory is available, the app clearly shows fallback guidance so you still have practical safety steps."

### 0:55-1:20 Evacuation centers tab
**Narration:**  
"Next, open Evacuation Centers. You can view nearby centers, status labels like Open, Full, Closed, or Unknown, and the map for quick orientation. If no center is found nearby, contact your barangay DRRM office."

### 1:20-1:40 Emergency hotlines tab
**Narration:**  
"In Emergency Hotlines, you get national emergency numbers plus available city and barangay contacts. You can tap the number to call on mobile. Always verify local numbers with your LGU."

### 1:40-1:55 Community report tab
**Narration:**  
"In Community Report, residents can submit updates such as evacuation status or road conditions to help improve community awareness."

### 1:55-2:00 Outro
**Narration:**  
"For immediate danger, call 911 and follow official LGU, PAGASA, and PHIVOLCS advisories."

---

## Synchronized Shot List

### 0:00-0:12
- **On screen:** Home page with app title visible.
- **Action:** Slow scroll or slight mouse movement to orient viewer.
- **UI target:** Header/title and top layout.

### 0:12-0:30
- **On screen:** Language selector and location picker.
- **Action:** Select one language, type a demo barangay, click one suggestion.
- **UI target:** `LanguageSelector`, `LocationPicker`.

### 0:30-0:55
- **On screen:** Guidance tab content.
- **Action:** Keep Guidance selected, slowly scroll through NOW/NEXT_24H/DURING_IMPACT/AFTERMATH cards if shown.
- **UI target:** Guidance summaries, action items, any fallback label.

### 0:55-1:20
- **On screen:** Evacuation centers tab.
- **Action:** Click `Evacuation centers`; pause on status badges and map.
- **UI target:** Center cards, distance text, status badge colors, map pins.

### 1:20-1:40
- **On screen:** Emergency hotlines tab.
- **Action:** Click `Emergency hotlines`; hover one hotline number and show categories (Emergency, Police, Fire, DRRM, Barangay).
- **UI target:** Hotline list, disclaimer, tap-to-call links.

### 1:40-1:55
- **On screen:** Community report tab.
- **Action:** Click `Community report`; show report type dropdown and message field (do not submit unless demo data is needed).
- **UI target:** Report form fields and submit button.

### 1:55-2:00
- **On screen:** Return to top section (or keep on hotlines/report with clear footer/disclaimer).
- **Action:** Hold frame steady for closing line.
- **UI target:** Safety-focused end frame.

---

## Recording Checklist

### Before recording
- Set browser zoom to 100% and use one clean tab/window only.
- Use a single demo location that has visible data across tabs (Guidance, Evac, Hotlines).
- Mute notifications and close unrelated apps.

### During recording
- Speak at ~130-145 words per minute to fit 2 minutes cleanly.
- Keep mouse movement slow and intentional; pause 1-2 seconds after tab switches.
- Do not rush through hotline numbers; keep them readable.

### Export
- Recommended: 1080p, 30fps, H.264 MP4.
- Target duration: 1:55 to 2:05.
- Add title card (optional): "How to Use the PH Disaster Preparedness Guide (2-Minute Walkthrough)".
